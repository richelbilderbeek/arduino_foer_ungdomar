# Stegmotorer

Power

12V + : red
12V - : black

Driver: ST-M5045

Wire on stepper motor|Connect to
---------------------|-------------
Black                | B- driver slot
Orange               | B+ driver slot
White                | A- driver slot
Yellow               | A+ driver slot
White-black          | White-orange stepper motor wire
White-red            | White-yellow stepper motor wire

Wire on Arduino  |Connect to
-----------------|-------------
12               | PUL+ (+5V)
13               | PUL- (PUL)
GND              | DIR+ (+5V)
GND              | DIR- (DIR)
